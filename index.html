Bio

Hi there! I am a double bassist and educator located in central Canada. I have been a member of the Winnipeg Symphony Orchestra since 2019

I currently also serve as Acting Principal Bass of the Regina Symphony. I've previously served as Principal Bass of the Des Moines Metro Opera and Assistant Principal Bass of the Carmel Symphony. As a guest artist I've appeared with the Manitoba Chamber Orchestra and A Far Cry, among numerous other ensembles

I have worked with numerous students through Yale's Music in Schools Initiative and the Winnipeg Symphony's Sistema program. I have served on the faculty of the Manitoba Conservatory of Arts and Music and the Douglas Kuhl School of Music. I hold degrees from Indiana University and Yale University

I teach privately both in person in Winnipeg and Regina, as well as virtually over Zoom. If you are interested in studying with me, please get in touch using the contact page.
